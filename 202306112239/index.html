MOSFET Transistor

A MOSFET transistor has several unique properties:

  • Can handle > 1 A of current
  • Dissipates a lot of heat, usually needs a heat-sink

How They Work

Examples

  • IRFZ44N N-channel TrenchMOS transistor
    • 55 V Max
    • 49 A MAx
  • IRF540 N-channel TrenchMOS transistor
    • 100 V Max
    • 23 A Max
