Flash STM32 MCUs w/ Open Source Software

  1. Download the latest GNU ARM Toolchain for Linux. Compression and Extraction - tar, gzip

  2. Download open-source stlink debugger from github.com/stlink-org/stlink.

  3. Configure your paths to the binaries. Adding Binaries to Path - Linux

  4. Connect your ST-Link to your MCU (3.3V, GND, SWDIO, SWCLK). Find your MCUâ€™s datasheet to find out which pins to connect to.

  5. Connect your ST-Link to your computer. Run st-info --probe. You should get the response: Found 1 stlink programmers. Otherwise, go back to step 1.

  6. Flash: sudo st-flash write <file_to_flash>.bin 0x08000000. Reset: sudo st-flash reset.
