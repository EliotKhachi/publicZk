Purpose of Quality Control

Quality control is regarded as the pursuit to maintain regularity and compliance to pre-determined standards with respect to a product or service. Quality Audits and Their Types

Achieving completely standardized work and elimination process variation within an organization is impossible, but every step is an improvement in… * Customer Satisfaction * Reduction of Cost * Reduced Scrap/Repair * Improved Cycle Time * Improved Employee Moral
