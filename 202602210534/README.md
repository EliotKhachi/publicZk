# Curry Recipe

- olive Oil
- Sautee 1.5 medium white onions  
- After turning gold  
- Remove chicken skin, add cut chicken thighs or cut breasts, or 10 drumsticks 
- Bay leaves  
- 2-3 tbsp curry powder, 1 tsp salt (or garlic salt) and 1 tsp pepper, 1 tsp paprika, 1 tsp red pepper powder (or chili flakes)   
- 1 cup Chicken broth + 1 cup boiling water + 2 tbsp tomato paste  
- Dice chunky 3-4 medium sized russet potatoes  
- 5 full garlic cloves  
- 1 medium sized lemon freshy squeezed
