Relays

Relays are Switches that are flipped by running current through them rather than by mechanically flipping them. Typically relays require a sustained current to stay flipped.

Types

  • SPST - Single Pole Single Throw (A.K.A non-latching)
  • SPDT - Single Pole Double Throw
  • DPDT - Double Pole Double Throw
