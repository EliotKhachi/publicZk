Drone Project

Build a quadcopter that can hover in the air.

Purpose

Learn core concepts and practical applications of embedded systems.

Components

1 X Blue-Pill Microcontroller (STM32F103C8T6)
1 X GY-521 Accelerometer & Gyroscope Sensor
1 X Mounting Plate 4 X DC Motors and Propellers
4 X Motor Holders
4 X Motor Drivers (MOSFETs)

Layout

The mounting plate should be large enough to mount all of these components. Top View Adding our components’ lengths, we get about 4.9“. We’ll make our mounting plate 6”.
Adding our components’ widths, we get about 3.7“. We’ll make our mounting plate 4.5”.
