Pane Commands in WSL

  1. Creating Panes
  • Alt + Shift + - creates a new vertical pane.
  • Alt + Shift + + creates a new horizontal pane.
  1. Closing Panes
  • Ctrl + Shift + w closes the focused/selected pane. If there is only one pane, WSL is closed.
  1. Resizing Panes
  • Shift + Alt + Arrow-Key resizes the panes depending on the direction of the arrow-key pressed.
