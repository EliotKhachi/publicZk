Components of a URL

Take the following URL: https://www.example.co.uk:443/blog/article/search?docid=720&hl=en#dayone

  1. Scheme https://

  2. Subdomain www.

  3. Domain Name example.

  4. Top-level Domain
    co.uk

  5. Port Number: 443
