Advanced High Performance Bus

This bus is used to access certain peripherals on the microprocessor, such as the GPIO pins. The bus must first be enabled through the AHBENR (Advanced High-Performance Enable Register) located under the RCC How to Output to a Pin - Types of Registers. The clock is also enabled through the AHBENR register for the port that we need, i.e. enable GPIOC for port c.
